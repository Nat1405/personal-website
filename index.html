Research

To date I've completed four research terms (two Co-ops) with two of them in chemistry and two of them in astronomy. My chemistry research used quantum chemical computer simulations to search for new Atomic Layer Deposition materials (a critical enabling technology for denser processors). My first astronomy research term used high performance linux machines to reduce terrabyte datasets to look for observational evidence of dark matter. My second astronomy research term in Binary planet formation used linux machines to reduce pettabyte ALMA> datasets to perform a Bayesian statistical analysis.
